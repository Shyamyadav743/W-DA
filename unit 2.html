Unit 2

Website Development Planning Process

Define the Website’s Purpose and Audience Determine the Website’s General Content Select the Website’s Structure Page Length, Content Placement, and Usability Develop a Plan to Test, Publish, and Maintain the Website

1. Define the Website’s Purpose and Audience

Define the website’s purpose, such as informational, e-commerce, educational, etc., and identify your target audience, such as their demographics and technical skills.

2. Determine the Website’s General Content

Identify the types of content the website will have, such as text, images, videos, etc., and develop a content strategy to ensure it flows logically and is engaging.

3. Select the Website’s Structure

Create a sitemap, define the navigation structure, and design wireframes for the website’s layout.

4. Page Length, Content Placement, and Usability

Decide on page length, ensure content is placed strategically, and ensure the website is user-friendly and accessible on multiple devices.

5. Develop a Plan to Test, Publish, and Maintain the Website

Develop a testing plan (functionality, browser compatibility), create a publishing plan (hosting, domain name), and plan for regular maintenance and content updates.
